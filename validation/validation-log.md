# Roof AI Validation Log

Permanent customer-discovery database for Roof AI.

## Validation Scoring Framework

| Signal | Definition | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Red | Low urgency, weak pain, no lead intent, or unclear homeowner need | Pause or reframe the message |
| Yellow | Clear pain but uncertain willingness to submit email/ZIP or use the tool | Keep testing and refine positioning |
| Green | Strong pain, repeated pattern, clear willingness to use Roof AI or request report | Prioritize in product and acquisition |
