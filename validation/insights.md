# Roof AI Insights

## Day 1 Summary

The strongest Day 1 signal is that homeowners want decision support and confidence, not only a cost calculator. The language of uncertainty is emotional: they worry about hidden water damage, bad contractor advice, and making the wrong repair-vs-replace call.
