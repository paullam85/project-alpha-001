# Roof AI Feature Ideas

## Day 1 Summary

Feature ideas should focus on reducing homeowner uncertainty before quote calls, not expanding the form too quickly. The strongest opportunities are decision guidance, risk scoring, and trust support.

## Seed Feature Ideas

| ID | Feature Idea | Problem Addressed | Related Insight | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Feature #001 | Repair vs Replace Advisor | Homeowners do not know whether repair is enough or replacement is smarter. | Insight #003 | Very High |
| Feature #002 | Roof Risk Score | Homeowners need a simple way to understand urgency and danger level. | Insight #005 | High |
| Feature #003 | Contractor Trust Checklist | Homeowners distrust conflicting or unclear contractor claims. | Insight #002 | Very High |
| Feature #004 | DIY Roof Confidence Checker | DIY homeowners worry whether roof work was sealed or installed correctly. | Insight #001 | High |
| Feature #005 | Hidden Leak Risk Assessment | Homeowners fear water damage they cannot see. | Insight #005 | High |
| Feature #006 | Roof Weight / Structure Calculator | Homeowners worry about material weight and structural capacity. | Insight #004 | Medium-High |

## Feature Scoring Framework

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Red | Interesting but weak evidence or not tied to lead conversion | Do not build yet |
| Yellow | Repeated concern but unclear willingness to use or submit lead | Test with content or copy |
| Green | Repeated high-importance pain with clear lead intent | Prioritize MVP iteration |
