# Roof AI Feature Ideas

## Day 1 Summary

Feature ideas should focus on reducing homeowner uncertainty before quote calls, not expanding the form too quickly. The strongest opportunities are decision guidance, risk scoring, and trust support.

## Feature Scoring Framework

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Red | Interesting but weak evidence or not tied to lead conversion | Do not build yet |
| Yellow | Repeated concern but unclear willingness to use or submit lead | Test with content or copy |
| Green | Repeated high-importance pain with clear lead intent | Prioritize MVP iteration |
