## Key Findings

- 140°F attic temperatures are often normal.
- Homeowners frequently misdiagnose normal attic heat as a roofing problem.
- Soffit intake is commonly blocked or ineffective.
- Ridge + Soffit is the dominant recommended configuration.
- Baffles are frequently overlooked.
- Radiant barriers receive strong support in hot climates.
- Attic fans remain controversial.
