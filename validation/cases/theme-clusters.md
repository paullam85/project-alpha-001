# Reddit Case Theme Clusters

## Ventilation & Attic Comfort

This cluster captures homeowner confusion around attic heat, roof ventilation, and comfort improvements.

### Included Themes

| Theme | Description |
|---|---|
| Professional Selection Confusion | Homeowners are unsure whether to call a roofer, HVAC contractor, insulation contractor, energy auditor, or home inspector. |
| Ventilation Configuration Confusion | Homeowners struggle to understand which vent types matter and how they work together. |
| Attic Heat Misdiagnosis | Homeowners may treat normal attic heat as proof of a roof or ventilation defect. |
| Airflow Principles | Balanced intake and exhaust matter more than the raw number of vents. |
| Ridge Vent | Commonly recommended exhaust path when paired with proper intake. |
| Soffit Vent | Commonly recommended intake path, but often blocked or ineffective. |
| Attic Fan | Controversial and situational; may help in some contexts but can create pressure or energy issues. |
| Radiant Barrier | Strong support in hot climates when installed correctly and paired with airflow. |
| Baffles | Frequently overlooked but important for maintaining soffit airflow. |
