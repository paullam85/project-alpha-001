## Key Findings

- Airflow matters more than vent count.
- Intake + Exhaust loop is essential.
- Ridge Vent + Soffit Vent is the most recommended solution.
- Attic heat does not automatically indicate a problem.
- Radiant barriers require correct installation and airflow.
- Attic fans are situational.
